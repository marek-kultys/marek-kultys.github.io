{{ site.data.pages.project-benevolent.tagline }}

At BenevolentAI I lead product design and user research activities. Currently, my focus is on target identification, one of the core areas of pre-clinical drug development. Our tech and products cover the entire process: from biomedical knowledge and reasoning, to molecular design.

{{ site.data.pages.project-biodesign.tagline }}

In 2017–2019 I led a desing and research team focused on helping life science companies design and build software products. We worked with Lifebit, Genomics PLC, Repositive, NatureMetrics, and RebelBio.

{{ site.data.pages.project-sequence-bundles.tagline }}

Between 2013–2016 I led the design and development of a novel method for visualising and exploring biological sequence data. Sequence Bundles were built, launched, and published in collaboration with EMBL-EBI.

{{ site.data.pages.project-ctrl.tagline }}

In 2014–2019 I led and contributed to a range of design projects at Ctrl Group, including intructional design for participants in a number of clinical studies, a conversational interface for patient management of depression, and a user-friendly report of patient health data.

{{ site.data.pages.project-immunocore.tagline }}

I worked with Immunocore between 2011–2013, just before they became a biotech super-star. Amongst other things, I designed Immunocore's logo which is still being used!

{{ site.data.pages.project-bento.tagline }}

In it's early product days, I helped to set out the vision, understand the users, and define the shape of the Bento Lab, the world's first personal DNA laboratory.

{{ site.data.pages.project-data-explorers.tagline }}

I authored and led a series of hands-on workshops for data science academics and professionals, introducing them to the principles and best practices of visual design.

{{ site.data.pages.project-mitosis.tagline }}

I created a set of visualisations explaining the cellular processes of mitosis and meiosis. Published under an open license, the diagrams became popular on Wikipedia pages.

{{ site.data.pages.project-lingua.tagline }}

This is my personal investigation into the limits of communication. I looked at historical and fictional instances of communicating with extraterrestrial intelligence to draw some scientific and existential conclusions.

{{ site.data.pages.project-sex-shop.tagline }}

I designed a logo for a fictional sex shop. Sexy, bold, and juicy.
