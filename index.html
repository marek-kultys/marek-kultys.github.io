I'm currently working at BenevolentAI where I help design tools for smarter and faster development of new medicines using AI.

Over the past 8 years I've worked with two biotech unicorns, and multiple health and tech companies. I designed products for clinical scientists, data sharing platforms, conversational interfaces, and information experiences for clinical trials.

Until 2018 I worked as a design lead at Science Practice and Ctrl Group. In 2014 I also led early design work for Bento Lab.

I also write about design, give talks, run design workshops, and serve as a mentor for startup teams.
