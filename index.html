Currently, I work as a design lead at BenevolentAI. Our growing team helps conceptualise, design, and develop new software products for AI-enabled drug discovery.

Over the past 8 years, I've worked with two biotech unicorns and a range of health and tech companies. I designed products for clinical decision making and reporting, for finding, sharing, and visualising biomedical data, and for running clinical trials.

Until 2019, I worked as a Design Lead at Science Practice and Ctrl Group. In 2014 I led early design work for Bento Lab.

Sometimes, I also speak at conferences, run design workshops, and serve as a design mentor.
