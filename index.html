Currently, I lead product design work at BenevolentAI where I build smarter and faster ways of discovering and developing new medicines using AI.

Over the past 8 years I've worked with two biotech unicorns, and multiple health and tech companies. My work included products in genomics, data sharing platforms, conversational interfaces, and information experiences for cinical trials.

Until 2018 I worked as a design lead at Science Practice and Ctrl Group. In 2014 I also led early design work for Bento Lab.

Apart from that, I also write about design, give talks, run design workshops, and help startup teams as a mentor.
